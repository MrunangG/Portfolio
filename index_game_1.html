Controls:

  • Press "S" to start the game, press "P" to pause the game.
  • Use arrow keys to move the spaceship(Player) left and right.
  • Press Spacebar to shoot bullets

Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to destory all the aliens before they kill all your 3 lives, or before they reach the bottom of the screen
  • The aliens move from right to left till they reach the end of the screen and then shift one unit below, and keep following the same movement loop
  • The movement speed of aliens is inversely proportional to their number, meaning the aliens move faster as their number reduces.
  • The game gets over when all aliens are destroyed


Logic Design and Implementation:

  • Use of custom bitmaps for all the aliens and the spaceship
  • Write proper logic for the movement of the aliens and the shifting of the aliens and their animation.
  • Use of simple logic to determine the direction of the movement for the alien horde
  • Randomly fire a bullet from the aliens that are directly in front of the spaceship(Player)
  • Use of object pooling for the bullets to optimize the code
  • Use of bunkers(again using custom bitmaps) for their drawing and animation(Destruction)
  • Create logic to determine when the game is over and display it on the screen.
